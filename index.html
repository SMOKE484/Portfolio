2. BarberFlow : Traditional barber shops use paper sign-in sheets, leading to chaotic queues and frustrated customers. BarberFlow is a digital barber shop queue management system designed to modernize the traditional paper-based process. It allows customers to join a virtual waiting list from their phones by entering their name and selecting their preferred barber (or opting for the next available). Barbers can view and manage the live queue through an intuitive dashboard, marking customers as "in service" or "completed" to keep the flow organized. The system provides real-time updates to customers on their queue position and estimated wait time. Shop owners gain access to an analytics dashboard that tracks peak hours, barber productivity, and service trends. Designed for simplicity, BarberFlow works on any device, whether it's a customer's smartphone, a shop tablet, or an admin's computer, ensuring a seamless experience for all users while eliminating the chaos of paper lists.
